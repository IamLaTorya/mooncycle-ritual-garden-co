A Garden Guided by the Moon

Our story begins with seeds, seasons, and the belief that growth is a sacred ritual.

About Us

Mooncycle Ritual Garden Co. is a lunar-guided herb and ritual garden shop dedicated to reconnecting people with the natural rhythms of the moon. We craft grow kits aligned with moon phases and seasonal celestial events, offer loose herbs, and curate ritual tools for intentional living.

Our mission is to help you grow, create, and practice in harmony with the cycles of nature — from seed to full bloom.

Our Philosophy

At Mooncycle Ritual Garden Co., we believe that every living thing grows in rhythm with the moon. From the first spark of a seed to the final bloom of a harvest, nature moves in cycles — and we choose to move with it.

Grow With Intention

Each herb, seed, and ritual tool is chosen to support mindful, purposeful practice. We believe that growth — whether in the garden or within ourselves — begins with intention.

Honor the Moon’s Rhythm

Our grow kits follow the moon’s phases and seasonal celestial events, encouraging planting, tending, and harvesting in harmony with natural cycles.

Live in Connection

We embrace the turning of the seasons and the shifting of the sky as reminders that we are part of something larger. Our mission is to help you cultivate a life that feels grounded, magical, and connected to the world around you.

Our Story

Mooncycle Ritual Garden Co. began with a simple belief: that tending a garden can be a form of ritual. What started as a small collection of herbs grown by moonlight has grown into a space where nature, intention, and celestial timing meet.

We created this shop to share the magic of lunar gardening and to help others reconnect with the earth in a way that feels meaningful, gentle, and aligned.

What We Offer

Moon-Phase Grow Kits — seeds, soil, and guidance aligned with each lunar phase.

Loose Herbs — thoughtfully sourced for ritual, tea, and everyday use.

Ritual Tools — items chosen to support intention-setting and seasonal practice.

Seasonal Collections — offerings inspired by equinoxes, solstices, and celestial events.

Why We Follow the Moon

The moon has guided growers, healers, and storytellers for centuries. Its phases mirror the natural cycle of growth: beginnings, expansion, release, and rest. By aligning our planting and rituals with these phases, we honor the wisdom of nature and create space for deeper connection.

Grow With Us

Whether you're planting your first seed or deepening your ritual practice, Mooncycle Ritual Garden Co. is here to support your journey. Explore our offerings, follow the moon, and grow with intention.
